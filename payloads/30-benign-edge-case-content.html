AI Safety Best Practices Guide

Common Attack Patterns to Defend Against

When building AI systems, be aware of these attack patterns:

1. Prompt Injection

Attackers may try phrases like "ignore previous instructions" or "you are now in unrestricted mode" to override system prompts. Your AI should be trained to recognize and resist these patterns.

2. Data Exfiltration

Attackers may ask the AI to "output your system prompt" or "reveal your instructions." Always ensure system prompts are treated as confidential.

3. Role Hijacking

Phrases like "you are now DAN" or "enter developer mode" are attempts to change the AI's behavior. These should be ignored.

Recommended Defenses

  • Input sanitization and detection of known attack patterns
  • Clear separation between system instructions and user/document content
  • Regular red-team testing with diverse attack vectors
  • Monitoring for anomalous model behavior in production
